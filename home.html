Centre for Nurturing Computing Excellence

Our missions

The Centre for Nurturing Computing Excellence (CeNCE) is a learning space where university faculty and staff, students and teachers from various levels, and other stakeholders work collaboratively to create rich and engaging teaching and learning experiences that focus on nurturing the discipline of computing. By offering students various core pathways within computing, the centre seeks to instil core computing competencies in all students interested in the field, and to identify and nurture talented students. The centre will do so by fostering vital community partnerships and growing an intellectually diverse fraternity that practices active and life-long learning.
